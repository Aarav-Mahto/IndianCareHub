What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, and other display preferences) over a period of time, so you donâ€™t have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the website. Without these cookies, some services and functionalities of the site may not work.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting information about how our site is used (e.g., which pages are visited the most). The data collected is anonymous and helps us improve the user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember your preferences (such as language or region) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: We use cookies to deliver relevant ads to users. These cookies track your browsing habits to help us show you advertisements that are likely to be of interest to you.

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly and provides a positive user experience.

  • Improve your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and interactions.

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use third-party cookies (such as Google Analytics) to collect usage statistics and help us understand how our website is performing. These cookies are managed by third-party service providers and are governed by their own privacy policies.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies you have and delete them on a case-by-case basis.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Block cookies from specific sites.
  • Block all cookies from being set.
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser.

For more information on how to manage cookies in popular browsers, please refer to the following links:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that if you choose to block or delete cookies, some parts of our website may not function as intended.
